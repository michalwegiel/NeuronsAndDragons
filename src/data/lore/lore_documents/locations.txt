LOCATIONS BIBLE
Below are the major regions, each with unique conflict hooks.

1. The Kingdom of Solara
Biome: Golden plains, warm climate
Tone: Heroic + political intrigue
Known for: Knights, fire-magic orders, ancient sky-city ruins
Major problem: Civil war brewing between two royal heirs
Key Mystery: A solar eclipse lasting 7 days foretells the return of the Emberlord.

2. Aethrindor (Elven Dominion)
Biome: Bioluminescent forests, floating canopies
Tone: Ancient + mysterious + tragic
Known for: Memory-trees, Resonance temples
Major problem: Elves are losing emotions; a curse from the Nightwhisper.
Key Mystery: A sealed ritual chamber whispers the voices of future selves.

3. The Khazdun Holds
Biome: Mountains, cavern megacities
Tone: Gritty + industrial magic
Known for: Aether-forges, rune-steam technology
Major problem: Deep tunnels cracking open into Rift zones.
Key Mystery: The Underdeep seal is weakening.

4. Arkin Wildrealms
Biome: Forests, tundra, cliff mesas
Tone: Tribal + spiritual
Known for: Totem magic, beast harmony
Major problem: Nature spirits going insane due to corrupted Aeon whispers.
Key Mystery: A mythic Beast-King returns every 500 years — and it’s overdue.

5. Serathi Enclave
Biome: Deserts, crystal canyons
Tone: Eldritch + scholarly
Known for: Illusion academies, sand-glass magitech
Major problem: A rogue faction summoned a Rift that now devours memories.
Key Mystery: They guard a prophecy written in a language that erases itself.

6. The Shattered Coast
Biome: Jagged islands, shipwreck fields
Tone: Dark fantasy + pirate frontier
Known for: Rift storms, mutated sea beasts
Major problem: A cult worships an unborn Aeon
Key Mystery: A sunken city sings during storms.
